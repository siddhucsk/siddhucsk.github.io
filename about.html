Résumé

I am siddharth. Currently a university student at christ university bangalore pursuing my bachelor of arts degree in Communication & Media, English and Psychology. When I am not a University student, I pursue my various interests from design, film making, minimalism, productivity sports, psychology and entrepreneurship.

I have had the opportunity to have lived in three cities namely Bangalore, chennai and mangalore. Although all located in the south of India, I have had different cultural, social and linguistic experiences I could learn from which have in turn shaped my personality.

I have attended two schools in my short lifetime. The schools I attended are Cambridge School, Mangalore and Sri Sankara Senior Secondary School, Chennai. Both schools have taught me a plethora of knowledge, nurtured relationships and instilled values. Both my schools were also an outlet to explore my creative pursuits, sports and other athletic talents.

Writing an about me page is challenging, given it requires downsizing bundles of data about myself into a couple words to keep the reader hooked. My roundabout solution is to divide it into sections, which will reduce your reading effort.
